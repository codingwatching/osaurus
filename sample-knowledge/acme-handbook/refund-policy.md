# Refund Policy

Acme offers refunds under the conditions below. This is the single source of
truth for support agents answering billing questions.

## Eligibility window

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase, no questions
asked. After 30 days, refunds are prorated based on unused subscription time.

## Non-refundable items

- One-time setup and onboarding fees.
- Usage-based charges that have already been consumed (for example, API credits
  that were spent).

## How a customer requests a refund

1. The customer emails **billing@acme.example** with their order number.
2. Support confirms the purchase date and eligibility window.
3. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within
   **5 to 7 business days**.

## Escalation

Disputes over $500 are escalated to a billing manager before any refund is
issued.
