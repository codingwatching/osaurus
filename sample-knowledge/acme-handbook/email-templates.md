# Support Email Templates

Reusable replies for the support team. This document **omits the `type` field on
purpose** so you can test the OKF validation button, which flags documents that
are missing a frontmatter `type`.

## Refund approved

> Hi {name}, good news — your refund of {amount} has been approved and will land
> on your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Thanks for your
> patience!

## Refund denied (outside window)

> Hi {name}, thanks for reaching out. Your purchase falls outside our 30-day
> refund window, so we're unable to issue a full refund. We'd be glad to look at
> a prorated credit — let us know.

## Password reset

> Hi {name}, you can reset your password from the sign-in page using "Forgot
> password." The link is valid for 60 minutes. Reply here if it doesn't arrive.
