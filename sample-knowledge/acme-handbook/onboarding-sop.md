# New Employee Onboarding SOP

Follow these steps for every new hire. The People team owns this procedure.

## Before day one

- Create the employee's account in the identity system and assign a manager.
- Order a laptop and ship it to arrive **two business days** before the start
  date.
- Add the new hire to the team calendar and the welcome channel.

## Day one

1. Manager sends a welcome message and shares the first-week schedule.
2. IT walks the new hire through account setup and two-factor authentication.
3. The new hire completes the security and privacy training module.

## First week

- Pair the new hire with an onboarding buddy for daily check-ins.
- Schedule a 30-minute meeting with each immediate teammate.
- Confirm payroll and benefits enrollment is complete by **Friday**.

## Sign-off

The manager marks onboarding complete in the HR system once all first-week items
are done.
